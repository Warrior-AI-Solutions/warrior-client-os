## Purpose

Most clients come to us knowing they have a problem. Very few know exactly what to build. Skill 1 closes that gap.

By the end of a Skill 1 session, we know:
- **Who** has the problem (avatar â€” specific, not generic)
- **What** the problem costs them today (in time, money, or quality)
- **What** the product needs to do (jobs to be done, not features)
- **What** the market looks like (how many clients could benefit)
- **What** the ROI looks like (the number that justifies the investment)
- **What** we are NOT building (anti-product â€” prevents scope creep)

This output becomes the input for Skill 2.
